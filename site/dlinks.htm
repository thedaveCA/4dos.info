DOS Batch Newsgroups

A good place to get help by knowledgeable people is the Usenet. Here are many DOS- and batch-related newsgroups, and people are glad to help as long as you post your question in the relevant newsgroup. Note that Google Groups or News-Reader.org allows you to read all Usenet groups and older messages in case they are not found on your provider's news server.  

Usenet can be a lively place, so be prepared: a necessary resource not to be without here is found at Flame Warriors Home and Andrew Heenan's Guide to Flaming. Also take a look at the Jargon File if unsure about the slang being used.

  • alt.msdos             Usenet Group: MS-DOS and batch discussions and help
  • alt.msdos.batch       Usenet Group: DOS batch discussions and help.
  • alt.msdos.programmer  Usenet Group: General DOS programmer's  discussions and help, much batch related material.
  • alt.msdos.batch.nt    Usenet group: NT batch discussions and help.
  • comp.os.msdos.4dos    Usenet group. 4DOS discussions
  • comp.os.msdos.misc    Usenet Group: General DOS problems.
  • comp.os.msdos.programmer  Usenet Group: General DOS programmer's  discussions and help, much batch related material.
  • comp.unix.dos-under-unix  Usenet Group: DOS emulation under Unix
  • de.comp.os.msdos      German Usenet Group: DOS and batch problems
  • ger.pc.dos            German Usenet Group: DOS and batch problems
  • microsoft.public.win3x_wfw_dos     Usenet Group: DOS problems under Win3.nn and Windows for Workgroups.
  • ms.public.win2000.cmdprompt.admin  Usenet group: NT5 command line problems.
