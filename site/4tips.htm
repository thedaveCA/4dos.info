4DOS Tips & Tricks
 

The 4DOS-Tips below contain the archives of the FIDO-net 4DOS board with batches and aliases 1992/1994. 

 

I have found very lovingly done compilations from the usenet group for the years 1996 - 1998, all with an index and readily decoded executables. Does anybody remember who did these compilations? I'd like to give them their due credit here.

 

It is really  a pity there are no such compilations for later years as well as  for the now defunct Compuserve forum, where Mike Bessy gave his highly interesting comments.

 

Then there are the archives of the 4DOS usenet group, which was, after FIDO and Compuserve, the main support forum for 4DOS users.

 

All these archives contain many aliases and batches,

 

If you find further material that would fit this site  I'll be happy to receive and perhaps include it here.
